Bridging the Safety Gap: A C-ITS Solution for Protecting Vulnerable Road Users

Authors: Mario Fiorentino, Michele Caggiano, Alessandro Magliacane, Angelo Coppola, Andrea Marchetta

Conference: 2025 55th Annual IEEE/IFIP International Conference on Dependable Systems and Networks - Supplemental Volume (DSN-S)

Date: June 23-26, 2025 | Location: Naples, Italy

DOI: 10.1109/DSN-S65789.2025.00043

Abstract: This paper proposes a C-ITS Platform for the Safeguarding of Vulnerable Road Users using camera-equipped RoadSide Units (RSUs), vehicle On-Board Units (OBUs), and Virtual-OBUs. The SALVM system is tested in a co-simulation HIL environment to detect imminent danger and send real-time notifications.

An AI knowledge base system for the recognition and personalised treatment of SARS-CoV-2

Authors: Beniamino Di Martino, Gennaro Junior Pezzullo, Alessandro Magliacane, Hung-Wei Li, Meng-Yen Hsieh

Journal: International Journal of Embedded Systems

Keywords: semantic; ontology; e-health; expert system.

DOI: 10.1504/IJES.2025.10073047

Abstract: The work described aimed to develop an expert system for personalised recognition and treatment of SARS-CoV-2. A clear methodology was defined, supported by conceptual diagrams to outline the logical flow of the problem and proposed solutions. This methodology is based on key components: a Bayesian network to calculate the probability of infection by analysing the patients symptoms, contacts, and geographic context; and a semantic component to determine the most appropriate treatment using the patients clinical and personal information, such as allergies or individual risk factors.

My latest projects

SALVM
The SALVM project aims to enhance road safety for vulnerable users through an intelligent C-ITS platform. By integrating advanced communication systems like C-V2X and AI, the system enables real-time communication between vehicles and infrastructure to provide timely danger warnings, reduce accidents, and optimize urban traffic flow.
Satellite Attitude Control
A simulation project developed in MATLAB and Simulink to model and control a satellite's attitude. It implements and compares two control strategies, PID and LQR, to stabilize the satellite's orientation and align it with a desired target, analyzing performance under different simulated space conditions.
Smart Parking IoT
An intelligent parking system based on an ESP32 microcontroller. This IoT project monitors vehicle entries and exits to manage parking space availability in real-time. A companion website, built with PHP, allows for remote monitoring of the lot's status and available spaces.
Electronic Register
A PHP-based web application designed to streamline communication between a school's secretariat and its students. The system allows administrative staff to upload grades, while students can log in to a dedicated portal to view their academic results and year-end status.
PASM: Business Process Optimization
A management software developed in Python for PASM, a precision mechanics company. The application is designed to optimize business workflows by managing customer orders and automatically generating corresponding invoices in PDF format, improving operational efficiency.
